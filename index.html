History

Black Turtle Ghana

Black Turtle Ghana is built upon a powerful legacy and a forward-looking vision. Our story begins with Ghana itself—the nation that first reclaimed its independence in Africa and ignited a continent-wide movement. Inspired by this history of self-determination, we stand on the cusp of a new, groundbreaking endeavor. At our core, we are the home of The Black Think Tank, a revolutionary initiative dedicated to research, development, and tangible empowerment. This space is designed to unite Africans and the global Diaspora, creating a powerful network to engage, innovate, and uplift one another.

Mission Statement

Ghana, the land that first reclaimed its independence and inspired a movement across Africa, is once again on the cusp of another ground-breaking endeavor- The Black Think Tank. A revolutionary space dedicated to research and development, this Think Tank will empower Africans and the Diaspora to engage, innovate, and uplift. The Black Think Tank aims to:

- Publish annual reports to keep the Diaspora updated on Ghana's economic progress.

- Foster collaboration around small business incubation.

- Navigate real estate investments in a cohesive manner.

- Promote sustainable agricultural economics.

- Connect with our brand's vision

When Steve Cokely Jr. envisioned the The Black Think Tank, he imagined a world where Africans, no matter where they are, could reconnect with their roots, harness their potential, and make tangible progress. By supporting The Black Think Tank, we are taking active steps towards:

- Equipping partnerships with local farmers.

- Equipping young entrepreneurs with digital skills, such as website development.

- Providing a nurturing environment for artists and writers.

- Ensuring safe land deal settlements.
